How to sign up and install Utraspeed apps

 

Get the app

To use the app, you must first download the utraspeed app on your smartphone.

 

Phone requirements

 Utraspeed app is available for iPhone and Android smartphones. Due to the fact the app requires a consistent connection, we support tablets that have sim card installed or wifi-only devices (for example, the iPod Touch).

 

You can use on Windows phones and Amazon Devices using our mobile site utraspeed.com

 

Utraspeed app works with all major cell carriers (like T-Mobile, Verizon, AT&T, Sprint, and spectrum) and most minor carriers (like Virgin Wireless, Metro PCS, or Cricket Wireless).

 

Other services may not be supported, so use your smartphone's phone number when creating an account.

 

 

How to install

1.      Use your phone to go to your app store (the iOS App Store for iPhone and Google Play Store for Androids)

2.      Search for "utraspeed" and install the free utraspeed app

3.      After a successful installation, proceed to sign up step. You are almost ready for your first utraspeed ride!

Sign up for a utraspeed account

Before you begin, be sure you have the following:

· Your phone number

· Your email address

· A photo of yourself

 Start

·         Type in your device's phone number

·         To verify your identity, we will send a verification code via text to your phone number. We want to make sure you are human!

·         The text message should arrive immediately. If you do not see it after a bit, tap 'Resend code.'

·         Type in your name, email address, and take a selfie so your driver knows who to pick up

·         That is, it! Once you have set up your account, you will be able to request a ride

How to synchronize your contacts

An easy way to search for destinations is by syncing the addresses from your contact list. This way, you can easily find contacts’ addresses when typing their name in the drop-off field.

You can then select the contact so the address will be set as the drop-off location. You can also add or delete a contact address, and it will automatically synchronize with your device’s contact list. Here is how:

1.      Tap ‘Search destination’

2.      Tap ‘Import contacts’ at the bottom of the list (this will pop-up if you have not yet granted access permission)

3.      Tap ‘Allow’ when prompted to give contacts list access

4.      Now your contacts should appear as search results in the drop-off field when you search by their name or address

5.      Tap a contact name to use the address as a destination

6.      You can also tap on the edit button next to the contact to add or remove an address–that is it!

Driving Requirements

All utraspeed drivers must meet certain requirements to drive on the platform. Applicant and vehicle requirements can vary depending on your city or State.

To start an application, see How to become a driver for instructions.

State and local requirement

Age requirements

The minimum age requirement to drive with utraspeed ranges from 21–25 and varies by region. See your State and City Driver Info for your area's age requirement.

Vehicle standards

All Utraspeed vehicles must have four doors and a minimum of five seatbelts (maximum of eight seatbelts).

Do not own a vehicle? Utraspeed's Express Drive program helps you rent a car so you can start driving.

Taxis and stretch limousines vehicles are not accepted on the Utraspeed platform.

Additional vehicle requirements vary based on your region's requirements.

Background check

Utraspeed conducts a criminal background check as a part of the application process, with your consent. Background checks can take several weeks to process and may be delayed due to court and DMV closures caused by COVID-19

For the background check, U.S. drivers must provide a valid social security number. Background checks are then run by a third-party company, Checkr, Inc. You can check the status of your background check by logging in to the Checkr applicant portal.

Utraspeed views and assesses your background check results to determine driver eligibility. You may be ineligible to drive on the Utraspeed platform if your background check results reveal that you:

· Are listed on the National Sex Offender Registry database

· Have been convicted of the following:

o    A disqualifying violent crime (such as homicide, kidnapping, human trafficking, arson, burglary, carjacking, robbery, or aggravated assault)

o    A sexual offense (such as rape, sexual assault, or child pornography)

o    An act of terror

o    Driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs in the past seven years (period may vary based on regional jurisdictions)

o    A disqualifying fraud-related offense in the past seven years (period may vary based on regional jurisdictions)

o    A disqualifying drug-related offense in the past seven years (period may vary based on regional jurisdictions)

o    A disqualifying theft or property damage offense in the past seven years (period may vary based on regional jurisdictions)

Utraspeed requires an up-to-date background check to remain on the platform. We will conduct continuous criminal and driving record monitoring of active drivers.

Utraspeed reserves the right to disqualify a driver at any time, should their background check reveal any disqualifying offense. If you believe that the contents of your background check are inaccurate or incomplete, you can contact Checkr through the Checkr applicant portal.

 

DMV check

U.S. drivers must undergo a driving record check which is run by a third-party company, Safety Holdings Inc.

The DMV check results are then assessed against Utraspeed’s internal safety criteria and applicable regulations.

Utraspeed may disqualify any applicant whose driving record reveals:

· Four or more moving violations in the past three years (such as accidents or traffic light violations)

· A single major moving violation in the past three years (such as driving on a suspended license or reckless driving)

· A DUI or other drug-related driving violation in the past seven years (period may vary based on regional jurisdictions

· A serious driving-related conviction in the past seven years (such as a hit-and-run or a felony involving a vehicle)

Utraspeed enrolls active drivers into DMV record continuous monitoring, which notifies us of new violations.

DMV requirements may vary depending on your region. Check your local state and city requirements page and stop by your local Hub for more details about region-specific DMV check requirements.

Utraspeed reserves the right to disqualify a driver at any time, in compliance with applicable law, should their driving record reveal any disqualifying information or for any other safety-related reason.

 

Driver license, license plates, and insurance

Utraspeed drivers must have the following:

· Current and valid driver’s license

· Valid plates with current registration (commercial plates are acceptable)

· Current and valid insurance with your name on the policy

Your region may not require your name to be printed on your insurance policy. If your insurance policy does not have your name listed, you will need to get a declaration page from your insurance company with your name.

Some cities and states require drivers to have in-state documents. See State & City Driver Information for required documents.

Community Safety Education program

Utraspeed's committed to educating our community on appropriate behavior in a Utraspeed ride. To help prevent unpleasant experiences, we have developed a Community Safety Education program.

Passenger payments

Passengers are charged an upfront price for their rides. This amount is based on the estimated time and distance of the ride, plus any service charge and applicable tolls.

 

Passengers may also be charged for cancellations and no-shows

 

This upfront price also includes Utraspeed’s service charge and any third-party fee. These charges do not impact the amount you earn as a driver and will not be reflected in your 'Driving payment History'.

.

Ride earnings for drivers

Your ride earnings are calculated based the ride type option selected and the region where the passenger is picked up.

This calculation includes a base fare, time, and distance.

Ø  Base fare: the amount you earn for starting a ride

Ø  Time: the amount you earn for every minute you drive

Ø  Distance: the amount you earn for every mile you drive

The calculation for your earnings starts one minute after you tap ‘Arrive’ in your app. If you pick up a passenger within one minute of arriving, the calculation starts when you tap ‘Pick up.’

After you cancel or drop off a ride, we no longer track the time and distance of the trip. If you accidentally cancel or end a ride early, you should ask your passenger to request a new ride.

Safety, policies, and accessibility:

 

 

Safety guideline and Rider policies for Utraspeed rides

We mindful of rider safety and we pride ourselves on our amazing community of drivers and riders. Here is a breakdown of our policies around the different uses for Utraspeed.

Children seventeen & Under

Children are welcome to join you in a Utraspeed ride, but they cannot ride without an adult.

You should plan to bring your own car seat for children that need one. Car seats must fit legal requirements in your state and city.

Wheelchairs

Riders with fixed-frame wheelchairs can turn on Access Mode to request rides. In regions where Access Mode is available, riders can request wheelchair-accessible vehicles.

All Utraspeed drivers are required by law and Utraspeed’s policy to transport riders who use foldable mobility devices.

 

Pets and service animals

Pets

We love your four-legged friends but not everyone is comfortable with having animals in their car. If you want to bring a pet along, call your driver once they accept your ride request.

Learn more about our pet policy.

Service animals

Utraspeed’s policy states drivers are required to accommodate riders with service animals.

Learn more about our service animal policy.

Bringing friends

You’re welcome to bring along friends on your ride. Pile in, buckle your seat belts, and enjoy the ride!

A standard Utraspeed ride can seat up to three riders. If you have a bigger group, a Utraspeed Comfort can seat up to five. As part of Utraspeed's Health Safety Commitment, the front passenger seat must remain empty.

Requesting rides for others

You're welcome to request rides for someone else. Just enter your friend's address in the app, then tap ‘Change rider’ at the top of the screen.

The driver and your friend will get details, and you can watch the progress of the ride in your app.

Learn more about requesting rides for someone else.

Running errands

We love seeing community members help each other out, so we see running errands as a great part of the Utraspeed experience.

When making multiple stops, keep in mind that drivers are just like you and may have other plans later in the day. Anytime you leave the driver's vehicle, please make sure to bring all your belongings with you. If your stop is going to take longer than 5 minutes, ask the driver to end the ride. Then, request a new ride when you are ready to go to your next destination.

 

 

Anti-Discrimination Policies

Discrimination against riders or drivers is not tolerated on the Utraspeed platform. Reports of discrimination based on any of the following can result in account deactivation:

· gender, gender identity or expression

· physical or mental disability (including the refusal of service animals or mobility devices)

· medical condition

· marital status

· age

· sexual orientation

· race

· color

· national origin

· religion

 

We are committed to maintaining an inclusive and welcoming community. Our mission is ensuring people who need rides most, can get them.

Refusing riders based on any of the protected characteristics listed above is a violation of our Terms of Service.

 

Foldable wheelchair policy

Drivers are required by law and utraspeed’s to transport riders who use foldable mobility devices. Drivers are required to assist riders in the storage of their mobility devices, unless physically unable to do so.

Mobility devices include the following:

· Foldable walker

· Foldable scooter

· Canes, crutches and other smaller assistive devices

· Foldable wheelchair

Wheelchairs and scooters that can safely and securely fit in the vehicle’s trunk or backseat, without obstructing the driver’s view, must be accommodated by drivers on the Utraspeed platform.

Drivers may not deny service to or otherwise discriminate against individuals with disabilities. Drivers who are found to have refused service due to a mobility device are at risk for removal from the platform for violating Utraspeed’s Terms of Service.

 

Service animal policy:

When it comes to transporting riders with service animals, drivers on the Utraspeed platform should remember one thing: Always Say Yes. You are required by the law and Utraspeed’s policy to always accommodate service animals, even if you have an allergy, religious or cultural objections, or a fear of them.

We know service animals can raise some questions for Utraspeed drivers, but they are essential for many people. Utraspeed drivers like you are in a unique position to help riders with service animals get around effortlessly in their community — thanks for taking this seriously and being there when people need your rides the most.

For advice on picking up rides, check out our 5-star driving tips in the Learning Center. To visit our Learning Center in the Utraspeed Driver app, open the main menu and tap 'Support and Safety,' then tap ‘Learning Center.'

Lost and found for riders

Lost phones

If you lost the phone associated with your account or are unable to contact your driver, call customer support or send email to customerhelp@utraspeed.com to inform us of your lost item.

 

We also encourage you to take any action necessary to fulfill your provider’s insurance claim requirements.

Contact the driver

When you report a lost item through the user app or the link in your email ride receipt, you will see the option to call or send a message to your driver. These options will be available for 24 hours after your ride ends.

For calls and messages, the utraspeed app uses a masked phone number so your personal contact info is hidden.

When leaving a message, we encourage you to provide the best contact number the driver can reach you to help make the return smoother.

 

Important notice

Utraspeed is unable to provide insurance, replacements, or reimbursement for lost items, we strongly recommend contacting your local authorities to file a police report if you have lost vital items with sensitive or personal information.

 If the police reach out to us with the proper and valid paperwork via email, we will assist them in any way we can. Check out our website for more information on police outreach.

Keep in mind that we are unable to guarantee items will be found or delivered immediately. Per Utraspeed's Terms of Service, we do not procure insurance for, nor are we responsible for, personal belongings left in the car by drivers or riders. For this reason, we are unable to provide refunds for rides or reimbursement for the value of lost items.

 

Return fee

As a compensation for your driver’s time, riders are subject to a fee of $15 for the return of a lost item if the driver must drive down to return the item. The full amount goes to the driver.

Customer will not be charged until the driver returns the item and notifies us; they have successfully returned your item. If you would like to thank your driver even more, an extra tip is a great way to do so. 

Note: Tips (in-app or cash) do not count toward the return fee. If an item is returned, a return fee will be charged to your ride.
